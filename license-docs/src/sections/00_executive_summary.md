## 1.2 The Anti-Capture Mechanism
The central innovation of this license is the **Anti-Capture Framework** (Articles II & X). It recognizes that traditional "Non-Commercial" clauses are insufficient against modern corporate structures that use shell companies, foundations, and "research labs" to launder commercial intent.

This license implements:
1.  **Identity-Based Exclusion**: Specific individuals (e.g., S.H. Altman, E.R. Musk) and their organizations are permanently barred.
2.  **Transitive Prohibition**: The ban extends to their families, trusts, and any entity they invest in.
3.  **Strict Liability**: Violations do not require "intent." If money flows to a Prohibited Party, the license is breached.
